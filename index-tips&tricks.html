General Tips & Tricks

Explore Everything

Don’t rush the main path. In Elden Ring, exploration is rewarded constantly with new weapons, spells, upgrades, and hidden bosses. If an area feels too hard, leave and come back stronger later.

Level Vigor Early

New players often focus too much on damage stats. Increasing Vigor gives you more health and makes mistakes far less punishing. Surviving one extra hit can completely change a fight.

Learn Enemy Patterns

Combat is about timing, not button mashing. Watch how enemies move, dodge at the right moment, and wait for openings. Patience is usually stronger than aggression.

Upgrade Your Weapon

A stronger weapon matters more than a few extra character levels. Visit blacksmiths often and use Smithing Stones to improve the weapons you enjoy using the most.

Use Spirit Ashes

Spirit Ashes can summon allies during many difficult battles. They help distract bosses and create breathing room, especially for beginners learning the combat system.

Dying Is Part of the Game

You will lose fights — a lot. In Elden Ring, every defeat teaches positioning, timing, and strategy. Treat deaths as lessons instead of failures.
