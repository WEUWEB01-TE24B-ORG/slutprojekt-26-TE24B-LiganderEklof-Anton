Builds For Beginners

Vagabond Strength Knight

Playstyle - Heavy melee fighter focused on blocking, guard counters, and staggering enemies with powerful hits. Very forgiving for new players and excellent for bosses early on.
Starting Class - Vagabond
Weapons - Lordsworn's Greatsword, Claymore, or Great Épée
Shield - Brass Shield or Heater Shield
Armour - Vagabond Knight Set or Banished Knight pieces
Talismans - Green Turtle Talisman, Curved Sword Talisman, Axe Talisman
Skills - Stamp (Upward Cut), Square Off, Lion's Claw
Stat Prioirty - Vigor > Strength > Endurance > Dexterity

Samurai Bleed Build

Playstyle - Fast aggressive katana build that inflicts Bleed quickly while maintaining strong mobility and dodge potential. Excellent against bosses with large health pools.
Starting Class - Samurai
Weapons - Uchigatana, Nagakiba, or dual Uchigatanas
Armour - Land of Reeds Set or Ronin-style medium armour
Talismans - Green Turtle Talisman, Winged Sword Insignia, Radagon's Soreseal
Skills - Unsheathe, Bloody Slash, Double Slash
Stat Prioirty - Vigor > Dexterity > Endurance > Arcane

Prophet Flame Caster

Playstyle - Mid-range fire caster that uses Incantations for strong burst damage while still having melee backup. Great for clearing groups and exploiting enemy weaknesses.
Starting Class - Prophet
Weapons - Catch Flame Seal setup, Morning Star, or Flame Art Broadsword
Shield - Beast Crest Heater Shield
Armour - Prophet Set or Fire Monk pieces
Talismans - Faithful's Canvas Talisman, Green Turtle Talisman, Radagon Icon
Skills - Flaming Strike, Sacred Blade
Stat Prioirty - Vigor > Faith > Mind > Endurance

Astrologer Glintstone Mage

Playstyle - Long-range spellcaster focused on deleting enemies before they can reach you. Extremely strong early game thanks to efficient sorceries.
Starting Class - Astrologer
Weapons - Meteorite Staff, Short Sword, or Moonveil later on
Armour - Astrologer Set or Raya Lucarian robes
Talismans - Graven-School Talisman, Radagon Icon, Stargazer Heirloom
Skills - Glintstone Pebble, Rock Sling, Carian Slicer
Stat Prioirty - Intelligence > Mind > Vigor > Dexterity

Confessor Holy Paladin

Playstyle - Balanced melee and Faith hybrid using buffs, healing, and holy damage while remaining tanky enough for close combat.
Starting Class - Confessor
Weapons - Broadsword, Lordsworn's Straight Sword, or Golden Halberd
Shield - Brass Shield or Blue-Gold Kite Shield
Armour - Confessor Set or Knight armour pieces
Talismans - Two Fingers Heirloom, Blessed Dew Talisman, Green Turtle Talisman
Skills - Sacred Blade, Golden Vow, Barricade Shield
Stat Prioirty - Vigor > Faith > Strength/Dexterity > Endurance

Hero Dual Axe Berserker

Playstyle - Hyper-aggressive dual wield build focused on jump attacks and raw Strength scaling. Deals massive burst damage early game.
Starting Class - Hero
Weapons - Dual Battle Axes, Highland Axe, or Greataxe
Armour - Hero Set or heavy armour with high poise
Talismans - Claw Talisman, Axe Talisman, Green Turtle Talisman
Skills - War Cry, Barbaric Roar, Determination
Stat Prioirty - Vigor > Strength > Endurance > Dexterity
