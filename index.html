Startpage

Welcome to your first step into the world of Elden Ring. Whether you are completely new to Soulslike games or simply looking for a smoother start, this guide is designed to help you survive and succeed in the Lands Between. Here you’ll find beginner-friendly advice on combat, exploration, leveling, equipment, and progression, all explained in a clear and practical way.

One of the biggest challenges for new players is choosing how to build their character. That’s why this site also features recommended builds for different playstyles, from powerful melee warriors to spell-casting mages and balanced hybrid setups. Each build is created with beginners in mind, focusing on strong early-game options and easy-to-understand progression paths.

No matter how difficult the journey may seem at first, the right guidance can turn frustration into adventure. Start exploring, learn the basics, and discover what makes Elden Ring such an unforgettable experience.
